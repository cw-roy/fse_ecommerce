I love coffee.

Coffee, the liquid gold that's often referred to as the nectar of the gods, is truly a lifesaver for many of us. It's like a warm hug in a mug that jumpstarts our engines in the morning and keeps us going throughout the day. In fact, some of us might argue that it's the only thing that stands between us and becoming a grumpy, sleep-deprived monster. It's so essential that if coffee disappeared from the face of the earth, we'd probably have to rename our planet "Depresso" or "Yawnington". So, let's all raise a cup to this miraculous elixir that makes life a little more bearable - Cheers to coffee, the beverage that makes our day, one cup at a time!

Just a cuppa

Plain and simple.

Light roast coffee is like the shy cousin of medium and dark roast coffee - it's lighter in color and has a delicate, subtle flavor profile that lets the natural bean flavors shine through. Perfect for those who value the unique quirks of different coffee varieties, and who want a coffee that won't slap them in the face with its boldness.

The Diner

Just like at Mel's.

Medium roast coffee is like the Goldilocks of coffee roasts, not too light and not too dark, but just right. It's the perfect balance of sweet and sour, and the moderate caffeine content won't leave you bouncing off the walls like a hyperactive kangaroo.

Donut Shop

Simply classic.

Donut shop coffee: the OG of basic brews. It's the coffee you drink when you just want something simple and easy to pair with your favorite sweet treats. It's not the fanciest of drinks, but it gets the job done and has a cult following of die-hard fans who can't get enough of its comforting taste. So next time you need a caffeine fix and a donut to go with it, donut shop coffee has got your back.

Caffe Latte

Not like the ones on every corner.

Ah, the latte. Like a hug in a cup - a warm, comforting blend of espresso, steamed milk, and frothy goodness that's perfect for cozy mornings or midday pick-me-ups. Plus, it's a canvas for latte art, so you can enjoy your caffeine fix and your art appreciation in one delicious package.

Very Black

Dark as sin.

Extra dark roast coffee is like the Lucifer of the coffee world - bold, intense, and not for the faint of heart. It's roasted for so long that it's practically a different species from its lighter-roasted cousins. If you're looking for a coffee that can handle a little bit of cream and sugar and still punch you in the taste buds, extra dark roast is your go-to. Just don't expect it to be delicate or nuanced - this coffee is all about power and strength.
